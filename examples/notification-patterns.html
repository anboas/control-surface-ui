Implementation notes

Reusable notification contract

Rules for adding top-right notification areas and alert inboxes.

  • Use .if-popover.if-notification-menu, .if-notification-btn, and .if-notifications for topbar inboxes.
  • Use .if-notification-item--unread, --warning, and --danger to communicate state without custom styling.
  • Keep mark-read controls inside the notification popover so data-if-notification-read can clear unread states and badges.
  • Separate urgent alerts, digests, quiet-hour rules, and channel preferences so notification fatigue can be designed intentionally.
